Magisk and KSUModule Privacy Policy

This privacy policy describes how Magisk and KSUModule (the "Website") collects, uses, and shares your personal information when you visit or use the Website.

Information We Collect

We may collect the following information when you visit or use the Website:

  • Log data: This includes information such as your IP address, device type, browser type, operating system, date and time of access, and pages you visit.
  • Usage data: This includes information about how you use the Website, such as the modules you download, the features you use, and the searches you perform.
  • Personal information: If you choose to contact us through email or another form on the Website, we may collect your name and email address.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Operate and maintain the Website.
  • Analyze how you use the Website to improve our services.
  • Respond to your inquiries and requests.
  • Send you marketing communications (if you have opted in).

Sharing Your Information

We may share your information with the following third-party service providers:

  • Web hosting providers.
  • Analytics providers.
  • Marketing service providers (if applicable).

We will not share your information with any third party for their own marketing purposes without your consent.

Your Choices

You have the following choices regarding your information:

  • You can choose not to provide any information to the Website.
  • You can opt out of receiving marketing communications from us.
  • You can request access to or deletion of your personal information.

Data Retention

We will retain your information for as long as necessary to provide the services you request and comply with applicable laws.

Security

We take the security of your information seriously and use appropriate security measures to protect it from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction.

Children's Privacy

Our Website is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and you believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new privacy policy on the Website.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at Gmail.
